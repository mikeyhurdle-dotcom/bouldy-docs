# Bouldy — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-25_

## Introduction

Bouldy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

Climbing session data (sessions, attempts, grades, routes, projects, training plans) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and synced to your Bouldy account via Supabase if you sign in.

If you create an account, we collect your email address via Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or email/password registration through Supabase Auth.

We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-first analytics — it collects anonymised, aggregated usage signals (such as which screens are opened and how often sessions are logged) with no personally identifiable information, no IP address tracking, and no cross-app identifiers. You can read TelemetryDeck's privacy practices at [telemetrydeck.com](https://telemetrydeck.com). We additionally use Apple's `os_log` for on-device debugging, which never leaves your device.

We do not sell, rent, or share any of this data with advertisers or data brokers, and Bouldy does not contain third-party advertising SDKs.

## How We Use Your Information

Your climbing data is used solely to provide the app's functionality — tracking sessions, calculating streaks and grade pyramids, powering widgets, and syncing across your devices.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Storage & Security

Local data is stored in SwiftData on your device and persisted to a shared App Group for widget access.

Account data is stored in Supabase (EU region) with row-level security enabled — you can only read and write your own rows.

Beta videos and milestone recordings, if you record them, are stored in Supabase Storage behind the same row-level security.

We do not store any payment information — all purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit.

## Your Rights

You can export all your climbing data as CSV files from the Manage Account screen in Settings.

You can delete your account and all associated data from the same Manage Account screen — this permanently removes your profile, sessions, attempts, routes, projects, and achievements. The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.

## Children's Privacy

Bouldy is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
