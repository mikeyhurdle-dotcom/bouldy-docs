## Common questions

**Where is my data stored?**
Locally on your device first (SwiftData), then synced to your Bouldy account in Supabase (EU region) if you sign in. Beta videos and milestone clips live in Supabase Storage with row-level security so only you can access them.

**How do I export my climbing data?**
Settings → Manage Account → Export My Data. A CSV is generated and shared via the system share sheet.

**How do I delete my account?**
Settings → Manage Account → Delete Account. The deletion is immediate and removes your profile, sessions, attempts, projects, and achievements. It cannot be undone.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Bouldy → Cancel.

**My pyramid looks empty / wrong.**
Make sure attempts are logged with a `send_status` of `flash`, `redpoint`, or `onsight` — only successful sends count toward the pyramid. Attempts and falls are tracked separately so the pyramid reflects what you've actually sent.

**The Apple Watch app isn't syncing.**
Open the Watch app on your iPhone, scroll to Bouldy, ensure "Show App on Apple Watch" is on. Pull-to-refresh on the Watch session list forces a re-sync.
